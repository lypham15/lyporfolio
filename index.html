    I am a UX designer based in Leidschendam, The Netherlands. Understanding user behaviour and coming up with solutions that can help improve user experience is my ambition. I also keep in mind that every design can make an impact, so I always try to put all my effort and passion into completing any product. Designing websites and applications as a UX (User Experience) designer is a captivating endeavour that combines creativity and empathy. The allure lies in the opportunity to craft digital experiences that seamlessly integrate functionality and aesthetics, ensuring users navigate effortlessly through interfaces. Through different projects with clients, I developed a keen interest in designing products taking user experience at the centre of all the designs. I am also eager to learn and experience new things in a young and enthusiastic working environment.
